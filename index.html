At Base Coffee, we are devoted to offering a consistent and outstanding specialty coffee experience that elevates your daily routine. Every process in the chain is carefully considered, assessed, and continually revisited.

Our craft

Roasted in Berlin, perfected for you.

Roasting in Berlin allows us to connect with certified Q graders, enabling us to deliver the perfect pour. We favour light to medium roasts so you can taste the natural character of every origin.

Our culture is rooted in passion, curiosity and dedication — constantly pushing the boundaries of innovation while setting new standards for quality in specialty coffee.
