Our Story

Less, but better.

Base Coffee began with a simple belief — that a great cup of coffee can be a quiet, mindful ritual worth returning to every day.

Who we are

A common language, spoken everywhere.

We've been inspired by different cultures across the world and can't wait to introduce you to our taste through one common language spoken everywhere — coffee.

Our culture is rooted in passion, curiosity and dedication. We strive to create an environment where every cup we serve reflects our commitment to quality and our love for coffee, becoming an integral part of the city's daily routine.

Base Coffee uses minimalistic colours and a Japandi aesthetic to provide a familiar at-home feel, where our community can slow down in a comfortable, calm space.

What we believe

Our values

01

Quality

Specialty grade only. We taste, assess and revisit every step so each cup is consistent and exceptional.

02

Mindfulness

We encourage awareness of the impact your choices have on your well-being. Slow down — be present with your cup.

03

Community

A place where people come together and invest in their daily rituals, connected by a shared love of coffee.

Our craft

From bean to cup

Every process in the coffee chain is carefully considered, assessed, and continually revisited.

01

Sourcing

We seek out exceptional beans and curate tastes from origins all over the world, with care for the people behind them.

02

Roasting in Berlin

Roasting in Berlin lets us work with certified Q graders. We favour light to medium roasts to honour each origin's natural flavour.

03

The perfect pour

Our baristas brew with precision and intention so the cup in front of you is the best possible expression of the bean.
